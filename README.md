A CRM APPLICATION FOR BANQUET HALL BOOKING

Project Overview
The CRM Application for Banquet Hall Booking is designed to simplify the management of banquet hall reservations and customer interactions.
This system allows administrators to efficiently manage customer details, hall availability, event bookings, and payments.
It helps reduce manual work and improves service efficiency through automation and analytics.
Features
Customer Management - Store and manage customer details such as name, contact information, and booking history.
Hall Management - Manage banquet hall details including hall capacity, facilities, and pricing.
Booking System - Customers can book banquet halls for events such as weddings, parties, meetings, and celebrations.
Event Scheduling - Schedule events and avoid double bookings by checking hall availability.
Payment & Billing - Generate invoices and track customer payments.
Dashboard & Reports - View booking details, revenue reports, and upcoming events through dashboards.
Technologies Used
Salesforce CRM
Flow Builder
Apex Triggers
Lightning App Builder
Reports & Dashboards
Modules
Admin Module – Manages system operations and hall details
Customer Module – Handles customer information and booking history
Hall Management Module – Manages banquet hall availability and details
Booking Module – Handles event reservations and scheduling
Payment Module – Manages billing and payment tracking
Analytics Module – Displays reports and statistics
